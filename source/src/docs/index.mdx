## Browser compatiblitity

The library requires the following built-ins:

| ES/DOM Features        | Chrome | Edge | Safari | Firefox | Opera | IE  |
| ---------------------- | ------ | ---- | ------ | ------- | ----- | --- |
| ES5 Collection         | 38     | 12   | 8      | 13      | 25    | 11  |
| Promise                | 32     | 12   | 8      | 29      | 19    | -   |
| Proxy                  | 49     | 12   | 10     | 18      | 26    | -   |
| AbortSignal (optional) | 66     | 16   | 12.1   | 57      | 53    | -   |
